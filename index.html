Real questions. Real answers.

Is this an internship or a job? +

No — and we won't dress it up as one. The residency is a learning-by-doing program. You contribute to real projects, get real mentorship, and produce real portfolio work. We do not promise employment, salary, or fake "experience letters." What we do promise: the skills, portfolio, and confidence that make you genuinely hire-able.

I'm a total beginner — is this for me? +

You need working Python and basic SQL. If you've built anything at all — a college project, self-study repo, one finished course — you're fine. We take you from there to production-grade. If you can't yet write a for-loop, spend 2 weeks on a foundations course first, then come back.

How much time will I need each week? +

Plan for 15–20 hours per week: two live sessions, build blocks, PR reviews, and independent building. The more you put in, the more you ship. Serious residents come out with 3–5 portfolio-worthy projects.

Is it fully online? Which cities? +

Yes — 100% online, live. Built for Tier 1, 2 & 3 cities across India. Recordings and notes always available. We've had residents from Bhopal, Jaipur, Kochi, Guwahati, Pune — location doesn't matter.

Why only 10 people per batch? +

Because attention is the entire point. With 10 residents we can review every PR, debug your architecture on a call, and actually know your work. Big cohorts are certificate mills — we're deliberately the opposite.

What does ₹49,999 cover? +

Everything — 12 weeks of live sessions, mentorship, weekly PR reviews, capstone, community access, recordings, mock interviews, résumé review, completion certificate. Early-bird is ₹49,999 (regular ₹59,999). EMI on request.

What if I don't like it after starting? +

You have a 7-day no-questions-asked refund. Join the cohort. Attend the first sessions. Meet the mentors. If it isn't what we promised, email us within 7 days — we return 100%.

When does the next batch start? +

Soon — and applications close the moment we hit 10 confirmed residents. Apply now to lock your seat. We interview every applicant briefly to make sure it's a fit both ways.

Do I get a certificate? +

Yes — an honest completion certificate, plus verifiable GitHub contributions and project artifacts you can send any recruiter.

Can I pay in installments? +

Yes. EMI available on request. Reach out at sourabh@datadives.in after applying.
